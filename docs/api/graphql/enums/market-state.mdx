The current state of a market


```graphql
enum MarketState {
  STATE_PROPOSED
  STATE_REJECTED
  STATE_PENDING
  STATE_CANCELLED
  STATE_ACTIVE
  STATE_SUSPENDED
  STATE_CLOSED
  STATE_TRADING_TERMINATED
  STATE_SETTLED
}
```


### Values

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_PROPOSED</b></code>](#) 
> The governance proposal valid and accepted
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_REJECTED</b></code>](#) 
> Outcome of governance votes is to reject the market
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_PENDING</b></code>](#) 
> Governance vote passes/wins
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_CANCELLED</b></code>](#) 
> Market triggers cancellation condition or governance
> votes to close before market becomes Active
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_ACTIVE</b></code>](#) 
> Enactment date reached and usual auction exit checks pass
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_SUSPENDED</b></code>](#) 
> Price monitoring or liquidity monitoring trigger
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_CLOSED</b></code>](#) 
> Governance vote (to close)
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_TRADING_TERMINATED</b></code>](#) 
> Defined by the product (i.e. from a product parameter,
> specified in market definition, giving close date/time)
> 

#### [<code style={{ fontWeight: 'normal' }}>MarketState.<b>STATE_SETTLED</b></code>](#) 
> Settlement triggered and completed as defined by product
>
